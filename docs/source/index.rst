Who This Book Is For
-------------------

This book is designed for individuals who want to:

- Understand how money and markets actually work
- Recognize economic cycles and incentives
- Evaluate investment advice critically
- Build long-term financial confidence

What You Will Learn
-------------------

By the end of this book, you will be able to:

- Explain what money is and why inflation exists
- Understand how stocks, bonds, and derivatives behave
- Evaluate options strategies and leveraged instruments
- Construct and manage a portfolio across life stages
- Identify poor or misleading financial advice
